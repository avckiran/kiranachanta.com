Overview:

Experienced in full stack web development and data analysis. Worked with AngularJS (Angular 1), React JS with Redux, with backend Node, Express, MySQL and MongoDB. Can create beautiful UI designs using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe XD. Strong exposure to AWS cloud environment (EC2, RDS) and web deployment.

Brief History:

After my bachelors degree in Computer Science, I started my career in 2010 as a Programmer in Tata Consultancy Services. I worked directly with business users on web interface embedding Cognos reports. I got experience working with data warehouses, creating views and providing them to the client's customized web portal through cognos. Later I promoted as Systems Engineer and got the responsibility to develop a web portal for another client from scratch with Cognos integration. I was instrumental in delivering the project under the timelines integrated with client's web interface using javascript components. After that I moved to Cognizant Technology Solutions. I have gained experience in web design and developed mobile-first web app as a proof of concept using AngularJS (v.1) and ChartJS, which compliments the existing data visualizations in Tableau.

These initial years of my work experience gave me a ground to learn and enhance my skills and personality by working on real world projects. Being a born voyager, however, I wanted to explore the world and sharpen my skills by doing a Masters degree. I wanted to explore more in Data Analytics and how it is impacting the businesses across the world. Thus, I enrolled at UT Dallas for my Masters.

In addition to learning from and interacting with bright minds. I got the opportunity to start my own company UNIBEES Inc. (a Delaware c-corp) in January 2016. It is a mobile application that provides information about local campus events, deals, and gigs for students in a real-time setting.This step challenged me to come out of my comfort zone and work hard which culminated in scaling the company from 2 founders to a team of around 10 (peak size) all the while managing 4 product releases. As the technical founder and design-centered guy, I spearheaded everything from logo, color-scheme, typography, wireframes... to the core technology of the company. Designed the first app using ionic framework (hybrid framework uses cordova and Angular1). Also an internal dashboard in MEAN stack to track effectiveness of the app. All the technology is hosted in AWS cloud environment using EC2, RDS, S3, DynamoDB and other features. We closed $250K funding and scaled nationwide. As of today, its available in 100+ universities with 30K+ active users. For the past few months, working on migrating the tech stack to the ReactJS (with Redux) and React-Native.
